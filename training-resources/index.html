Career Options

  • Desktop Support

    Careers in desktop support are ubiquitous--most companies need some form of tech support. As an IT Support Special, your job duties will include things like troubleshooting user issues and helping with system upgrades. Explore the training options for this type of career below.

  • IT Network Engineering

    An IT network engineer designs the computer networks that organizations rely on to access, share, and store information. The complexity of the work depends on the organization: a small start up may employ a computer engineer to oversee a handful of desktop computers, while an engineer at a large multinational manages a web of complex, globally distributed networks. Check out the resources below!

  • Web Developer

    There are a lot of possible careers in development. For more information on the training available for these, check below!
