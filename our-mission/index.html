Our Mission

Hackertunity, Inc.

Hackertunity, Inc. is a non-profit in the process of being established. We’re based in California-- many of us with roots in the Antelope Valley. Our mission:

To break cycles of poverty, empower at-risk populations, and diversify the computer science field by providing training and resources for building community-owned technological solutions.

Essentially, we want to give people who could really use it an opportunity to jump-start a new career in tech. We planned to start by partnering with local high schools in the Antelope Valley, targeting at-risk students. We wanted to host a hackathon—an event where they would code their own apps in teams. However, our priorities shifted when the needs of the world shifted.

Now we’re focusing on highlighting available resources that could help people with what they’re dealing with around COVID-19. A lot of people have lost their jobs, and even their businesses, and m any are unsure of the future of their industries in general. Our focus is to provide resources to people who need a shift in their career, or even just need a new and innovative way to quickly make a livable wage during these uncertain times.

For more information on the resources that may help, check out the links in the COVID-19 section.
